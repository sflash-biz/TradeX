Editing the Greeting & Signature
Editing the e-mail greeting & signature is done through the Templates > E-mail Greeting & Signature function in the control panel. There are two fields you can edit in this dialog:
    Greeting - Enter the text that will be added to the beginning of every e-mail message sent by the software. This is a good place to put a simple greeting for your e-mail messages.
    Signature - Enter the text that will be added to the end of every e-mail message sent by the software. This is a good place to put your signature, a privacy notice, a link to your site, etc.
